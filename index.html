    Services

    Support to Vessels

    Protective Agency|Husbandry Services:

    We ensure that your vessels visiting Nigeria receive top-tier support and uninterrupted operations. Our team’s expertise in port operations guarantees seamless interaction with relevant authorities, ensuring your vessel’s smooth stay in Nigeria.

    Supply of Ship Spares and Stores:

    We provide timely and cost-effective procurement services for ship spares and equipment, including filters, motors, pumps, safety equipment, and marine accessories, ensuring minimal downtime for your vessels.

    Security Escort Vessels and Armed Guards:

    Protect your vessels from piracy and other security threats with our reliable onboard armed security services, including stowaway prevention and patrols.

    Safety Assessments and Inspections

    Inspection of LSA/FFA Equipment:

    Our certified team conducts thorough inspections of Life Saving Appliances (LSA) and Fire Fighting Equipment (FFA), ensuring your vessel complies with international safety standards.

    Terminal Safety Inspections

    We ensure third-party storage terminals meet your Health, Safety, Security, and Environment (HSSE) requirements through detailed audits and assessments of technical standards, environmental compliance, and operational protocols.

    Marine Compliance Consultancy and Ship Management

    Ship Registration with Flag/Class

    We streamline ship registration, classification, and documentation processes to ensure compliance with international maritime regulations, allowing you to operate smoothly in global waters.

    SMS Development and Review

    Our team helps develop and review Safety Management Systems (SMS) that comply with ISM Code requirements, ensuring safe and efficient vessel operations.

    Vessel Chartering/Brokerage Services

    With an extensive industry network, we provide access to a wide range of vessels for hire or purchase to meet your operational needs.

    Audits and Inspections

    ISM-ISPS-MLC Audits

    Our accredited inspectors conduct thorough ISM, ISPS, and MLC audits to ensure vessels and maritime companies meet international safety, security, and labor standards.

    Pre-Purchase Inspections

    Make informed investment decisions with our detailed pre-purchase inspections, ensuring that your maritime assets meet all operational and safety standards.

    ISO 9001:2015, 14001:2015, and 45001:2018 Audits

    We provide comprehensive audits to assess compliance with industry-leading ISO standards for quality, environmental, and occupational health and safety management.
